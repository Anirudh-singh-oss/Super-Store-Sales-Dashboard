# Super-Store-Sales-Dashboard
Super Store Sales Dashboard

* 🔹 Key Business Insights
  
> Strong Overall Performance
Total sales reached 522K with a profit of 68K, indicating a healthy profit margin and stable business growth.

> Customer Segment Contribution
The Consumer segment (44%) is the highest revenue driver, followed by Corporate (35%) and Home Office (21%).
→ Focus marketing strategies on high-value consumer customers.

> Payment Behavior Trends
Cash on Delivery (43%) is the most preferred payment mode, followed by Online (36%) and Cards (22%).

→ Opportunity to promote digital payments for better cash flow management.

> Shipping Mode Analysis
Standard Class (96K) dominates shipments, while Same Day delivery (11K) is least used.
→ Faster delivery options are underutilized and can be promoted for premium customers.

📈 Sales & Profit Trends

* Seasonal Sales Growth
Monthly sales show an upward trend towards year-end (Nov–Dec), indicating strong seasonal demand.

* Profit Fluctuations
Profit shows variability across months, suggesting cost or discounting impacts.
→ Requires better cost control during low-profit periods.


🌍 Regional Insights
* West Region Performance
The West region appears to generate higher sales compared to others.

→ Can be treated as a benchmark for performance strategies in other regions.
